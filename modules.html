+Vida Modules

Nutrition

Focus on proper diet and nutrient intake:

  • 2 Meals per Day + 2-3 Antioxidant Fruits
  • Breakfast with Coffee, Bread & Eggs
  • Hydration: 2-3 Liters of Water
  • Avoid Sugars and Processed Foods

Exercise

  • Walking 3km / 4x per Week or 5km / 2x per Week
  • Stretching & Flexibility
  • Strength Training
  • Posture & Mobility Exercises

Mental Training

Focus on exercises and strategies to enhance your mind:

  • Meditation Techniques
  • Memory Expansion
  • Focus & Attention Boost
  • Neuroplasticity Drills
  • Problem Solving Skills

Emotional Balance

Strengthen your emotional intelligence and mental resilience:

  • Stress Management
